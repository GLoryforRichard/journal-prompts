## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Journal Prompts ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our website at journalprompts.org. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

**Effective Date: March 16, 2026**

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Journal Prompts is designed with privacy as a priority:

- **No account registration** is required to use our service.
- **No personal information** such as your name, email address, or phone number is collected through the use of our journaling tools.
- **Your journal writing is never sent to our servers.** All writing you create using our prompts is saved exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage) on your own device. We have no access to it whatsoever.

## Information Collected Automatically

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as:

- Pages visited and time spent on site
- General geographic region (country/city level)
- Browser type and device category
- Referral sources

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It helps us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve the experience. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

## Cookies

Our site may use cookies for:

- **Analytics cookies** (Google Analytics) to understand site usage
- **Advertising cookies** (Google AdSense) to serve relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the core functionality of our journaling tools.

## Data Storage and Security

Since your journal entries are stored only in your browser's localStorage:

- **We cannot access, read, or recover your writing.** It exists solely on your device.
- **Clearing your browser data will permanently delete your saved entries.** We recommend exporting or backing up any writing you wish to keep.
- **No journal data is transmitted over the internet** through our service.

## Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any user data to third parties. The only third-party services we use are:

- **Google Analytics** for anonymous site analytics
- **Google AdSense** for advertising

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **support@journalprompts.org**.
